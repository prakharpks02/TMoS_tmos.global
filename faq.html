Getting Started

  • How do I partner with TMoS ?

    You can click on this link and get started. Or you can write to us at info@tmos.global

  • Are there any registration charges to use TMoS ?

    No, there are no registration charges to use TMoS.

  • Can I place multiple orders at once ?

    Yes. TMoS offers you the feature to place multiple orders at once. You can manage all your orders with a single dashboard.

  • What things can I get delivered with TMoS?

    We deliver over 100 types of items like groceries, vegetables & fruits, agro-products, food, packaged food, medicines, fashion and accessories, and more.

  • Is API integration needed ?

    You get free API integration with TMoS. Most of our clients opt for API integration. However, you can decide to manually use the dashboard to place your orders.

Payments

  • What payment methods can I use ?

    You can pay by card, net banking, or UPI to recharge your TMoS wallet or directly pay by using the preferred payment method.

  • What is the limit of TMoS wallet ?

    There is no limit to add money in your TMoS wallet. You can add as per your need.

Guide

  • How to live track my orders ?

    We offer a live-tracking feature to track your orders. Go to the dashboard and click on the respective order number to track and know its status.

  • How do I report an issue ?

    You can use a contact form or connect with us through this page.

  • How to generate receipts of my orders ?

    We provide an automatic receipt generation feature without any extra cost. You can use it from the dashboard.

  • What if I missed a verification call from TMoS ?

    In case you miss a verification call from our support staff, write to us on info@tmos.global or call on +91-8879138857.

  • Where can I get an estimated cost for my order ?

    You can use our Cost Calculator feature to see the detailed cost for each order.

Technical Guides

  • My page is not loading corectly ?

    In some rare instances, the server is not able to update static files in the browser due to caching. Press Ctrl + Shift + R

  • How to access APIs ?

    The APIs can be accessed after logging into the TMoS Platform and then clicking the API button on top.
